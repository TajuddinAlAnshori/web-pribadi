About

Hello, my name is Moch. Tajuddin Al Anshori. I was born in a small town in East Java, namely Mojokerto. I continued my studies at ITPLN. My hobbies are designing and editing such as making UI UX Design, Cinematic Videos, posters, etc. short story about my city, namely Mojokerto. the second smallest city in Indonesia is not much different from other cities in Indonesia. because Mojokerto is the place of the former Majapahit kingdom. there are many relics of the Majapahit kingdom such as the brahu temple, the sleeping buddha statue, the mouse temple, etc. Typical food from Mojokerto is onde-onde. In Mojokerto, there are many natural tourist attractions in the Mojokerto Regency area, namely in the villages of Pacet and Trawas. there is a very beautiful place with a cool atmosphere. such as Coban Rondo Waterfall, Dlundung Waterfall, Ubalan Waterpark etc.
